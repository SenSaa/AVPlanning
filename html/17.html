The network architecture was a deep network with 4 convolutional layers and 3 fully connected layers with a total of just under 10k parameters. For comparison, state of the art image classification architectures have 10s of millions of parameters. At every episode, a curved lane to follow is randomly generated, as well as the road texture and lane markings. The agent explores until it leaves the lane when the episode terminates. Then the policy optimises based on collected data and we repeat.
